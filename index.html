About SPREE 2023

SPREE 2K23, an annual extravaganza at PBR VITS College, is the ultimate fusion of technology, creativity, and collaboration. It's a one-day event where students can unleash their talents through an array of technical and non-technical competitions. What sets SPREE 2K23 apart is our commitment to inclusivity, as we warmly welcome colleges from across the region to join the competition. It's a day to connect with like-minded individuals, learn, and celebrate the richness of diversity.
